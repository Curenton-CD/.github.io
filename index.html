Personal Touch

I am so glad that you could join me, I have had the advantage of being able to experience absolute joy in my life. A really humble individual actually, and quite funny to! I grew up in a military family, I was able to live and travel around The U.S and Europe, meet people from all walks of life, learn new languages and see some of the most beautiful landscapes this world has to offer. I also learned how to survive, over-come obstacles, and trust my gut. I currently stay in Maryland with my husband, kids and dogs. I am family oriented so I visit my grandparents and parents as often as I can. I enjoy cooking, making cocktails, as I am also a Licensed Bartender and Caterer. I attend Norfolk State University, with just two semesters left I will obtain my B.S in Health Services Management my focus is Long-Term Care and rehabilitation. I look forward to working with a local sports team as an analyst. While I do enjoy catering and working in the Food and Beverage Industry, I have a knack for analyzing, and working with numbers, so I did my research on programs and bootcamps that offered data analytics, and in September of 2020, in the midst of a global pandemic and other uncontrollable life altering circumstances, I graduated from Thinkful's Data Analytics Immersion Bootcamp. I survived the long hours of studying and working on projects and attending lectures. And I am truly amazed with all I have learned, the people I've worked with and the programs I have mastered. I love working with data, and creating visuals to showcase what I've found, and using Excel, SQL and Python amongst others to find it. So much so, that outside of work, just for personal curiousity I often search through all types of data on sites like Kaggle, Jeremy Singer-Vine, data.world and medium. This past year, I became one of the Incorporators for Gamma Phi Lambda Sorority, Incorporated, a 501c3 here in Maryland. We have a Vision and Mission catered to and dedicated to Women's Empowerment, Community Service, and Sisterhood. We all individually own business that cater to our community in the form of Beauty & Cosmetology, The Arts, Fashion & Entertainment, Healthcare and Wellness, and Education. We want to teach others and support each other in leading with LOVE in our own personal lives.
